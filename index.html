At Kitchen Treasures, we know that you will settle for nothing but the best when it comes to your family. And this promising attitude is what inspires us every day. With a fine range of products and relentless quality checks, we too do our best in taking care of you and your loved ones. And we believe, until today and for years to come, this will be our greatest strength & our most valuable treasure.

Purity, simplicity & honesty. That has been and will continue to be our commitment to our consumers and our community.

Our Story

Kitchen Treasures comes from the house of Synthite – the world’s largest producer of value-added spice ingredients that has a legacy of over 50 years. 36-time winner of the ‘Exporter of the Year Award’ from the Spices Board of India, Synthite has always lived by its promise of delivering 100% quality, purity, and authenticity. Synthite’s unparalleled raw material sourcing ability is due to its highly organised farmer networks across the country and high level of control at the source.
